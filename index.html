SAN Group Hotels

Experience Comfort in the Heart of Mysuru

SAN Group Hotels proudly presents Hotel Maurya Palace and Hotel Maurya Residency, offering comfortable stays in the vibrant city of Mysuru. Located just minutes away from the iconic Mysore Palace, our hotels combine convenience, comfort, and warm hospitality for both business and leisure travelers.

Founded by Sri Nagaraj Prasad, Chairman of SAN Group of Hotels, the group has grown with a strong commitment to quality service and guest satisfaction. Today, along with his sons Mr. Sunil Kumar and Mr. Anil Kumar, the group continues to welcome guests with trusted hospitality and modern amenities.

Whether you are visiting Mysuru for business, tourism, or family occasions, SAN Group Hotels ensures a relaxing stay with excellent facilities, dining experiences, and personalized service.
