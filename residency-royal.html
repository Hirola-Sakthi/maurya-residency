Residency Royal (AC)

Premium Air-Conditioned Room

The Residency Royal AC Room at Hotel Maurya Residency is thoughtfully designed to provide guests with a premium and comfortable stay. These air-conditioned rooms feature plush bedding and a choice of king-size bed or twin beds, ensuring maximum comfort throughout your stay.

Guests can enjoy modern amenities such as a flat-screen TV with mirror casting, complimentary Wi-Fi connectivity, and an in-room coffee maker, allowing them to relax and refresh at their convenience.

With elegant interiors and a calm atmosphere, Residency Royal rooms offer the perfect combination of comfort, convenience, and modern hospitality.

Check Out

House Rules

  • Guests must present a valid government ID at check-in.
  • Outside visitors are not allowed in guest rooms after permitted hours.
  • Smoking is allowed only in designated areas.

  • Management reserves the right to admission.

Children & Extra Beds

  • Children below 6 years can stay free when using existing beds.
  • Extra beds can be arranged upon request with additional charges.
  • Families traveling with children can request suitable bedding arrangements.
