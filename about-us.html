About Us

SAN Group of Hotels is a well-known hospitality group based in Mysuru, Karnataka, offering comfortable stays, quality dining, and trusted hospitality for travellers from across India. Established with a strong vision for service and excellence, the group continues to welcome guests with warmth, comfort, and convenience.

The group was founded by Sri Nagaraj Prasad, a respected figure in the hotel industry and Chairman of SAN Group of Hotels. During a challenging time for the hospitality sector, his dedication, hard work, and passion for service laid the foundation for a successful hotel group in Mysuru. With the support of his sons, Mr. Sunil Kumar and Mr. Anil Kumar, the group has grown steadily while maintaining its commitment to guest satisfaction.

Today, SAN Group proudly operates Hotel Maurya Palace and Hotel Maurya Residency, both located in the heart of Mysuru, just a short distance from the iconic Mysore Palace and other major attractions. Our hotels are designed to provide comfortable accommodations with modern amenities, making them ideal for both business and leisure travellers.

Meet our Founder

Nagraj Prasad

Founder

Established during a period of challenge for the hotel industry,our founder put in an immense amount of effort towards creating his hotels alongside his sons Mr.Sunil Kumar and Mr. Anil Kumar. Our founder’s vision quickly gained support from the town’s people and brought in many well-known corporate clients and businessmen from various regions of India. On August 15, 1990, the Maurya Palace, a hotel with Ac and Non Ac rooms, opened its doors to the public. Along with his hotel, our founder opened one of the finest multi-cuisine restaurants called Jewel Rock, a true jewel for Mysoreans. The SAN Group brought in specialized chefs from all across the continent to create mouth watering delicacies for the people of Mysore. This includes North Indian, Chinese, tandoor, and continental cuisine. They also specialize in various pastries and desserts. Frequently Asked Questions

Where are SAN Group Hotels located? ▾
SAN Group Hotels are located at Sri Harsha Road, Mandi Mohalla, in the heart of Mysuru, Karnataka.
How far are the hotels from Mysore Palace? ▾
Both Hotel Maurya Palace and Hotel Maurya Residency are approximately an 8-minute walk from Mysore Palace.
What types of rooms are available? ▾
Guests can choose from AC and Non-AC rooms at both hotels, designed to provide comfort and convenience.
Do the hotels provide free Wi-Fi? ▾
Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available for all guests.
Are there dining options available at the hotel? ▾
Yes, the hotels feature in-house restaurants including Jewel Rock and Veg Kourt offering a variety of cuisines.
Do you offer catering services for events? ▾
Yes, SAN Hospitality and Services provide catering for weddings, corporate events, parties, and other celebrations.
Is parking available at the hotel? ▾
Yes, complimentary parking is available for guests.
How far is the railway station from the hotel? ▾
Mysore Junction Railway Station is approximately 2 km from the hotel.
