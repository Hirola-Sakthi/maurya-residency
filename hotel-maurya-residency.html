Hotel Maurya Residency

Hotel Maurya Residency, a proud unit of SAN Group Hotels, offers a comfortable and peaceful stay in the heart of Mysuru. Designed to provide a relaxing experience for both business and leisure travelers, the hotel combines modern amenities with warm hospitality.

Conveniently located near the famous Mysore Palace, the hotel provides easy access to major attractions, shopping areas, and transport hubs, making it an ideal place to stay while visiting the city.

Our Rooms

Hotel Maurya Residency features 24 well-appointed rooms, thoughtfully designed to ensure comfort and convenience for every guest. The rooms are categorized into Residency Deluxe (Non-AC) and Residency Royal (AC).

View Details
Residency Deluxe – Non AC Rooms

The Residency Deluxe rooms are designed to provide a relaxed and comfortable stay in a contemporary setting. Guests can choose between a king-size bed or twin beds based on their preference.

Each room is equipped with modern amenities including a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi connection, and a coffee maker, ensuring a convenient and enjoyable stay.

View Details
Residency Royal – AC Rooms

The Residency Royal rooms are air-conditioned and designed with soothing interiors to create a calm and comfortable atmosphere.

These rooms feature plush bedding, a flat-screen TV with mirror casting, free Wi-Fi connectivity, and a coffee maker, allowing guests to enjoy a refreshing cup of coffee or tea anytime. Rooms are available with king-size or twin beds to suit guest preferences.
